HTML map Tag

The HTML <map> tag defines an image map. An image map is an image with clickable areas. Areas are defined by one or more <area> tags.

Here is an example of using the <map> and <area> tags:

In the example above, the "workplace.jpg" image has three clickable areas:

  • The rectangular area around the computer links to the Wikipedia article about computers.
  • The rectangular area around the phone links to the Wikipedia article about telephones.
  • The circular area around the coffee cup links to the Wikipedia article about coffee.

You can use the <map> and <area> tags to create interactive and informative images.
